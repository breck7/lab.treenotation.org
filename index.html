The Tree Notation Lab

The Tree Notation Lab is an open source distributed research group started in Hawaii, on a mission to eliminate unnecessary complexity, by creating a self-sustaining Tree Notation ecoystem, for the advancement of cancer and medical research, and the general improvement of science worldwide.
